Home

Welcome to Loudwater Weather, the local weather station for Loudwater. This website is still in production and may change occasionally. The weather station uses a Raspberry Pi Pico 2 WH and an AM2320 sensor to measure temperature and humidity. Time and date are handled by JavaScript.

Thank you for using Loudwater Weather and I hope you find it useful and will continue to use it in the future.

This website is completely human made and no AI was used to create it.

Where is Loudwater?

Loudwater is a small village just outside High Wycombe in Buckinghamshire. The map pin is placed on the local Premier Inn. Passing through Loudwater is the River Wye chalkstream which is the 5th longest river in the UK and stretches 134 miles. Because it is a chalkstream, it serves as a lovely habitat for wildlife and there are less than 300 left in the whole world.

Live Weather

This page displays live weather data including temperature, humidity, and time. If the station is off, temperature and humidity values show the last known readings. When the station is on, the readings update roughly every 20 seconds. I've calculated a roughly 10 seconds delay between it being updated and it being displayed on the page.
